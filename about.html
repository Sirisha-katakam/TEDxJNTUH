About

TED is a nonprofit, nonpartisan organization dedicated to discovering, debating and spreading ideas that spark conversation, deepen understanding and drive meaningful change. Our organization is devoted to curiosity, reason, wonder and the pursuit of knowledge — without an agenda. We welcome people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world and connection with others, and we invite everyone to engage with ideas and activate them in your community.

TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, but today it spans a multitude of worldwide communities and initiatives exploring everything from science and business to education, arts and global issues. In addition to the TED Talks curated from our annual conferences and published on TED.com, we produce original podcasts, short video series, animated educational lessons (TED-Ed), and TV programs translated into more than 100 languages. Thousands of independently run TEDx events take place yearly, bridging communities globally. Through the Audacious Project, TED has catalyzed over $3 billion in funding for meaningful change. Recent initiatives include Countdown (climate action) and TED Democracy (vibrant futures).

About

In the spirit of discovering and spreading ideas, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized. (Subject to certain rules and regulations.)

About

TEDxJNTU Hyderabad is a student-led initiative at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad, that aims to bring together innovative thinkers, inspiring speakers, and passionate individuals to share ideas worth spreading. Our mission is to create a platform for students and the community to engage with thought leaders, explore diverse perspectives, and foster a culture of curiosity and learning.
